Recycle Kerala - Election Campaign Ad

Co-directed by our founders Dinjith Ayyathan and Rakesh Mantodi, the advertisement was made for DYFI’s 2021 election campaign. The video tells a heartwarming and uplifting tale about a bunch of young children from rural Kerala taking part in DYFI’s Recycle Kerala initiative by informing the authorities about scrap lying unattended in their neighborhood. The video was launched by K.K Shailaja, who was the Health Minister of Kerala at that time. The video received a huge response on social media, gained hundreds of thousands of views and was shared across various social media platforms.
